Hick's Law

MCDONALD'S

https://www.mcdonalds.com.ar/

Hick's Law suggest that the more options you give the user, the longer it takes for the user to make a decition. McDonald's take full advantage of this concept by creating a meaningful picture and giving the user just one option, which is just 'Conoce m√°s' (it means Learn More). By doing this they will optimize the use of the visitor's desire to know at least a little more of a potencial discount and will spend more time browsing on the website.
